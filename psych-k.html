What happens during a PSYCH-K session

During the session, we identify a particular situation that is causing you difficulty, break it down, address it and then transform the stress around it through different processes. When we identify stress within our system and remove it, we optimize our belief system. Stress is pressure, and negative emotions are distress, so if we have too much stress in our system, we can get into a state of overwhelm. Modifying our subconscious beliefs is the most effective way to support ourselves.

We use muscle testing technique to verify information from your subconscious and your superconscious.

To conclude the session, we take new beliefs or goal statements, and through our PSYCH-K® process, we internalize them into our subconscious mind.
